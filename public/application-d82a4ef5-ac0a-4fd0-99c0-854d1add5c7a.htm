Bringing the Power of Artificial Intelligence to Your Lawsuit

How It Works

  • 1. Where to Start?

    You need to sue someone or you have been sued.

    Gather everything you need to tell your story: write out the facts, gather the pictures, gather the documents, write out the names and contact info for all witnesses, everything you need to tell your story.

  • 2. Making the Call

    Fill out the intake form on this website. Set a time for a CourtDox rep to call you. The rep will ask you to tell the story and will ask you detailed questions about what happened, the witnesses, the documents and pictures in your possession, etc. Answer all of the questions as best as your are able.

  • 3. What Happens Next?

    CourtDox generates the documents needed for your lawsuit (powered by CaseCreate). We will respond to you within in 48 hours with access to your client portal (powered by Lawmatics) containing the documents we have generated for you and instructions on how to use them.

    PLEASE REVIEW YOUR DOCUMENTS! You are responsible to the court for the accuracy of the filings you make. CourtDox strives to give you the best possible documents according to the information you have provided but, ultimately, NO ONE KNOWS YOUR STORY BETTER THAN YOU!

  • 4. Need Additional Help?

    CourtDox can provide paralegals and attorneys on a CONSULTATION ONLY basis. Once you have completed that request process, the attorney or paralegal will review the documents for which you have requested review. 

    Paralegals - Review for formatting, clarity, and detail. Our contract paralegals will cite directly to your evidence if we have it and are able to understand it.

    Attorneys - Will research law for your matter and cite to law for your filings as well as editing the filing for clarity and content. Law cited will NOT be generated using AI. Our contract attorneys know how to do real research with real resources to obtain real cases in your state to support your position.
